Enshrined On-Chain AI Models enable native smart contract interactions with AI models.

## Overview

Traditional blockchain networks treat AI models as external resources, accessed through oracles or off-chain services. This creates a disconnect between smart contracts and the AI models they interact with, limiting the potential for truly decentralized AI applications.

Ritual takes a fundamentally different approach by making AI models first-class citizens on the blockchain, enabling atomic interactions between smart contracts and models. Just as smart contracts revolutionized digital coordination, enshrined AI models enable a new paradigm of programmatic AI interactions with cryptographic guarantees.

## Key functionality

### Native Model Representation
Models exist as on-chain objects with:
- Verifiable provenance
- Modular model authenticity
- Direct smart contract integration

### Atomic Contract-Model Interaction
Enables powerful primitives like:
- Model inference as part of transaction execution
- Conditional transactions based on model outputs
- Model-driven state transitions

### Decentralized Infrastructure
Built on Ritual's core components:
- Modular storage layers for model artifacts
- Verifiable execution through ZK, TEEs, and more
- Native model marketplaces to track and trade models

## Current State

Available today:
- Model registration and verification
- Basic model ownership primitives
- Verifiable inference execution

Coming soon:
- Integration with Symphony for scaling

## Use cases

- DeFi + AI: Automated market making with ML models, model-driven portfolio rebalancing, risk assessment for undercollateralized lending.
- Governance: DAO proposal analysis, automated policy enforcement, sentiment-driven treasury management.
- Gaming: NPC behavior models, dynamic content generation, player behavior analysis.
- Creator Economy: Generative art models, content moderation, personalization engines.
