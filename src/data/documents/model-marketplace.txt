## The IP Challenge

As AI models become increasingly valuable assets, there is a growing need for decentralized marketplaces that can:

- Uniquely identify and track models on-chain
- Verify model authenticity, provenance, and attest to correct inference execution
- Enable model monetization
- Protect intellectual property rights
- Reward model creators for their contributions
- Facilitate model fundraising, ownership, and trade

However, building such marketplaces presents significant technical challenges, particularly around model verification and IP protection.

## Ritual’s Model Primitives

Ritual introduces foundational primitives that enable verifiable model marketplaces:

### Enshrined AI Models

By natively enshrining AI models, we enable:

- Models to become first-class citizens on the blockchain
- Direct smart contract integration and ownership of models
- Seamless, verifiable inference execution without relying on off-chain services
- Native support for model versioning and updates, backed by blockchain provenance
- Decentralized model registration and tracking
- Model storage across multiple storage layers, ensuring consistency and accessibility
- Native fundraising and monetization mechanisms
- Enshrined fee distribution by model usage and ownership, with native DeFAI integration

### Verifiable Model Execution

Via Ritual’s advanced research into architecture primitives like `vTune` and `SVE`, we enable verifiable model execution:

- Statistical verification of model inference, fine-tuning, and training
- Proof of model authenticity and provenance
- Protection against model forgery
- Preservation of inference quality

### Model Monetization

Ritual introduces a new vault-like architecture for enshrining AI models and other IP primitives on-chain, in a way that is transparent, composable with DeFAI primitives, and builds an on-chain, verifiable tree of provenance and ownership:

- Purpose-built to support community-owned model fine-tunes and variants
- Trickle-up/down royalty distribution mechanisms from base models to child variants
- Automated usage tracking and payment distribution for models
- Enshrined representations of original models; prevents model piracy
- Model governance and treasuries built on top of DeFAI primitives

## Empowering IP Protocols

Ritual’s architecture provides the foundation for next-generation IP protocols. Two examples of protocols that can leverage Ritual’s capabilities include Story Protocol and Sentient:

**Story can leverage Ritual to:**
- Register AI models as IP Assets
- Enable programmable IP licenses for models
- Automate royalty distribution
- Enforce usage terms on-chain

**Sentient can leverage Ritual to:**
- Create verifiable model marketplaces
- Enable transparent model governance
- Support decentralized model development
- Facilitate secure model monetization
